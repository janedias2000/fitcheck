# Interview 7 — Annie

**Date:** June 2026
**Round:** 2 (Pricing + Concept Validation)
**Interviewee:** Annie, 53, former fashion stylist
**Length:** Phone interview

---

## Key Quotes

> "Even when I know what I'm doing, I still want someone to tell me it looks good."

> "You can know all the rules and still not feel certain — you just want confirmation."

---

## Key Takeaways

**Background:**
- Former professional fashion stylist — extensive style knowledge and industry experience
- Despite her background, still seeks external validation when getting dressed
- This is critical: even expert-level style knowledge doesn't eliminate the need for honest feedback in the moment

**Shopping behavior:**
- Relies on trusted network for confirmation, not for instruction
- Has developed strong style knowledge over decades — the gap is real-time confirmation, not education

**Concept reaction:**
- Positive — saw the value even with her level of expertise
- Understood that the product fills a different gap than style education

**Van Westendorp pricing:**
- Too cheap: below ~$5
- Good deal: ~$10–14
- Getting expensive: ~$20+
- Too expensive: above $35
- $9/month: Yes

**Relevance to venture:**
- Challenges the assumption that the product is only for women still building their style formula
- Even women who "know all the rules" want confirmation — the need for honest feedback is universal, not just a beginner problem
- Widens the potential customer base: problem doesn't disappear with experience

## Target Customer Assessment
**Broader signal target** — not the primary demo (53, expert) but confirms the problem is universal across experience levels, not just for beginners.
